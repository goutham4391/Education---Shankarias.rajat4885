    Free Civil Services Mock Interview

    Shankar IAS Academy offers free IAS/UPSC mock interview preparation and interview guidance program for all civil service aspirants who cleared UPSC mains. The mock interviews and personality test are under the guidance of retired,serving officers in Chennai, Bangalore, Delhi and Trivandrum centers.

    Feedback will be given by the eminent panelist indicating the strength and weakness of the students who face the mock session.

    Why You Should join IAS/UPSC Mock Interview Programme:

    • Pre Mock (SWOT Analysis) sessions by SIA Interview Experts Team
    • SWOT analysis is specifically suited for aspirants with prior interview exposure looking for score upgrade
    • Personalised DAF profiling sessions.
    • Interview centric expert lectures by domain experts
    • Interview mentorship programme - Candidates are assigned to personal mentors(Young Bureaucrats)
    • One-on-one sessions.
    • Mock Interview by serving and retired bureaucrats.
